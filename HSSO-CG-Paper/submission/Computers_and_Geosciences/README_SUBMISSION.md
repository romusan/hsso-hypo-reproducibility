# Computers & Geosciences submission package

This directory contains the editable manuscript source, compiled review PDF,
cover letter, required highlights, separate vector figures, and the cited
quality-control supplement.

## Upload mapping

- `manuscript/main.tex` and `manuscript/references.bib`: manuscript sources.
- `manuscript/main.pdf`: double-spaced review copy with line numbers.
- `cover_letter.tex` and `cover_letter.pdf`: editable and rendered cover letter.
- `highlights.txt`: five highlights, each within the 85-character limit.
- `figures/Figure_1.pdf` to `Figure_3.pdf`: separate vector artwork.
- `supplementary/Supplementary_Table_S1.csv`: cited QC supplement.
- `supplementary/Supplementary_Material_Captions.txt`: supplement caption.
- `data_statement.txt`: submission-system data statement.

## Required author actions before upload

1. Verify the corresponding-author email in the manuscript, cover letter, and
   Editorial Manager record (`romulo@ecci.edu.co`).
2. Create a DOI-backed archival release and replace the provisional archive
   wording in `main.tex` and `data_statement.txt`.
3. Generate the competing-interest `.docx` with Elsevier's declarations tool.
4. Confirm that the manuscript is not under consideration elsewhere and that
   the final author order, affiliations, ORCIDs, CRediT roles, funding, and AI
   disclosure are accurate.
5. Enter any requested reviewer suggestions directly in Editorial Manager.
